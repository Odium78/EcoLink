Short, research-backed content and live indicators that show how Climate Action (SDG 13) links with Clean Water (SDG 6), Life Below Water (SDG 14) and Life on Land (SDG 15).

How these SDGs are connected

SDG 13 (Climate Action) is the driver — without tackling climate change, water, ocean, and land systems will collapse.

SDG 6 (Clean Water) is the foundation — clean water sustains both people and ecosystems.

SDG 14 (Life Below Water) and SDG 15 (Life on Land) are the natural pillars they are the ecosystems that support all life and absorb climate impacts.

SDG 6 — Clean Water

Water scarcity and contamination change habitats on land and in the ocean, while altered water cycles intensify climate impacts.

SDG 13 — Climate Change

Warming alters precipitation and sea levels, driving drought, floods, and coastal erosion that directly affect both terrestrial and marine ecosystems.

SDG 14 & 15 — Life Below Water & Life on Land

Loss of forests and coastal wetlands reduces carbon sinks and biodiversity, weakening resilience to climate change and harming water quality and fisheries.
